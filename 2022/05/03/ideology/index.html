What is a mindset?

Created2022-05-03Updated2022-05-21

What is a mindset? It is an attitude. It can determine the direction of a culture as well as a society. In China, Marxism is the cornerstone of the mindset which help us understand the inner logic of things from a cross-section. As time goes by, it is important to update Marxism to meet the needs of the general public. Doing so will put everyone on a common ground and strengthen cooperation to meet greater challenges.

China needs to study Marxism and use it to build an advanced think tank, a social science and a philosophical system. With more specialized people, we can solve more complex issues.

At the same time, China will also focus on spreading Chinese culture and telling the Chinese story. The most wonderful mountain top views are always shrouded in heavy fog. Therefore, storytellers need to climb high enough to pass through these mists. To do so, China needs to strengthen its capacity, expand its reach, and make sure it is objective and truthful. China will also clean up cyberspace, eliminating rumors and disinformation while accommodating different points of view.
