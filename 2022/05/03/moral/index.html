What is the moral?

Created2022-05-03Updated2022-05-04

Different people have different personalities, and so does the country. China places great importance on a person’s morality. When a person has a higher level of morality, he or she can hold the right attitude towards history, nation, country and culture. Moral consists of two parts, one is a person’s knowledge and the other is his or her character.


First of all, the intellectual aspect

We need to believe in science and make it more accessible to everyone. For the traditional Chinese culture, we can keep the good parts and discard those that are against common sense.

In terms of personal character

We need to encourage everyone to pursue progress, maintain integrity, have a sense of responsibility, a work ethic, and a willingness to do something for the common good, while respecting everyone.

When the moral of a person is improved, a country will be stronger and the whole world will be more progressive. Therefore China values the education of moral.
