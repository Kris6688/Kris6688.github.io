What is the ideology?

Created2022-05-03Updated2022-05-04

What is an ideology? It is an attitude. It can determine the direction of a culture as well as a society. In China, Marxism is the cornerstone of ideology. In the new era, it becomes very important to make Marxism updated with more Chinese characteristics and to meet the needs of the general public. Doing so will put everyone on a common ground and strengthen cooperation to meet greater challenges.

China needs to study Marxism and use it to build an advanced think tank, a social science system, and a philosophical system with Chinese characteristics. With more specialized people, we can solve more complex issues.

At the same time, China will also focus on spreading Chinese culture and telling the Chinese story. To see the most wonderful view often needs to see through the heavy fog. Therefore, storytellers need to climb high mountains to get out of these mists. To do so, China needs to strengthen its communication capacity, expand its reach, and make sure it is objective and truthful. China will also clean up the cyber space, eliminating rumors and disinformation while accommodating different points of view.

What is the moral?

Created2022-05-03Updated2022-05-04

Different people have different personalities, and so does the country. China places great importance on a person’s morality. When a person has a higher level of morality, he or she can hold the right attitude towards history, nation, country and culture. Moral consists of two parts, one is a person’s knowledge and the other is his or her character.


First of all, the intellectual aspect

We need to believe in science and make it more accessible to everyone. For the traditional Chinese culture, we can keep the good parts and discard those that are against common sense.

In terms of personal character

We need to encourage everyone to pursue progress, maintain integrity, have a sense of responsibility, a work ethic, and a willingness to do something for the common good, while respecting everyone.

When the moral of a person is improved, a country will be stronger and the whole world will be more progressive. Therefore China values the education of moral.

What is the value?

Created2022-05-03Updated2022-05-04

Values are equally crucial to social development. Socialist core values represent the spirit of China in the new era.

So what is socialist core values?

The core socialist values contain three dimensions, which give direction to the future of the country, society and every citizen respectively.

From the aspect of the country
Prosperity, Democracy, Civility, and Harmony

The best evidence of this is the Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics, where China presented the world with a spectacular Olympic events, so if you haven't seen it yet, be sure to check it out online.

From the aspect of the society
Freedom,Equality, Justice, and Rule of law

This is a group of people in China who make delivery their profession, riding electric bicycles to be reviewed by the national leaders and every viewer in front of the TV screen in Tian’anmen Square. You might think that only military personnel have such opportunities. But these service industry professionals are true proof of the good life now.

From the aspect of the individual
Patriotism Dedication Integrity Friendship

China is a country with a long history, and it also has many excellent traditional cultures. As a member of the country, every Chinese person loves his or her own country and at the same time embraces cultures from other countries. Every Chinese is willing to establish friendship with others and introduce Chinese culture to their friends.
